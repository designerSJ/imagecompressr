How it works

1

Pick photos

Choose one or many images with the Android photo picker. The app only ever sees what you select.

2

Choose a goal

Tap a preset, or set a quality, target size, or exact dimensions. Watch the estimated size update live.

3

Save or share

Compress, compare before and after, then save to your gallery or share — all without leaving the app.

Your photos never leave your device

All compression happens locally on your phone. Image Compressr doesn’t upload your images, doesn’t require an account, and its core features work with no internet connection at all. The only network use is for ads and the related consent prompt.
