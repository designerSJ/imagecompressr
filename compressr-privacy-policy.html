Privacy Policy for Image Compressr

Last updated: 19 June 2026

Image Compressr (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is an Android image-compression app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, and how it is handled. By using Image Compressr you agree to the practices described here.

Summary

  • Your photos never leave your device. All compression and resizing happens entirely on your phone. We do not upload, store, or transmit your images anywhere.
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or account details. Image Compressr has no sign-in and no user accounts.
  • The only network activity is for advertising, provided by Google AdMob, and the associated consent flow.

How the App Works

When you select photos using the Android photo picker, the app reads them only to compress or resize them on your device. The results are saved to your device’s gallery or a folder you choose, or shared via your own choice of app. The core functionality works fully offline and requires no internet connection.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Your images or their contents.
  • Image metadata (such as EXIF location). The app can strip this metadata locally; it is never sent to us.
  • Personal identifiers, contacts, location data, or account information.

Advertising

Image Compressr displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain information from your device, which can include:

  • Advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID).
  • Device information (e.g. device model, operating system, language, general/coarse location derived from IP address).
  • Ad-interaction data (e.g. ads shown, taps).

This data is collected and used by Google in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy. For more on how Google uses data from apps that use its services, see “How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.”

Consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland)

Where required by law (for example under the GDPR), Image Compressr uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before serving personalized ads. You can review or change your advertising consent at any time from the app’s Settings → “Manage ad consent” option, where available.

Permissions

  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID — allows access to the Advertising ID, used by AdMob to serve ads.
  • Storage access on older Android versions (API 28 and below) is requested only to save compressed images to your gallery. On newer versions, no storage permission is required.
  • Photos are accessed through the Android system photo picker, which grants the app access only to the specific images you choose.

Children’s Privacy

Image Compressr is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Because the app does not collect or store your personal data or images on any server, there is no such data for us to retain. Compressed images remain only where you save them on your own device.

Your Choices

  • You can reset or limit ad personalization via your device’s settings (Settings → Google → Ads).
  • You can manage advertising consent from within the app where the consent form is available.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop all data processing by it.

Changes to This Policy
